Fettuccine Carbonara

Another popular Italian pasta that uses eggs and cured pork as in main ingredients for its sauce. Its flavor is rich and decadent mainly come from the perfect combo of eggs, cheese and bacon (and its lard) that make people can't stop eating it.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack fettuccine
  • 8 slices bacon, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clover garlic, minced
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook fettuccine in boiling water until al dente, about 12 minutes.
  2. Cook bacon in large skillet over medium heat until browned, about 10 minutes
  3. Cook onion and garlic until fragrant and put the bacon back into the skillet.
  4. Add the fettucine into the skillet and toss to coat the fettuccine
  5. Add beaten eggs and cook until the eggs barely set then quickly add the Parmesan cheese.
  6. Served it warm on the plate.
